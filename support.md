# ExoKeeper Support

## About ExoKeeper

ExoKeeper is a professional exotic pet management tool designed for reptile, amphibian, arthropod, and small mammal keepers. It provides feeding records, reminders, temperature/humidity tracking, growth curves, expense tracking, medical records, breeding management, and more — all in one app.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. ExoKeeper does not upload your data to any external server. Pro subscribers can optionally enable iCloud sync to keep data in sync across their devices.

### How many pets can I manage?

The free version supports up to 3 pets. You can unlock unlimited pets by subscribing to ExoKeeper Pro.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. ExoKeeper supports exporting your data as PDF reports and CSV spreadsheets. Data export is available for Pro subscribers.

### I lost my data after reinstalling the app. Can I recover it?

If you had iCloud sync enabled before uninstalling, your data will be restored automatically when you sign in with the same Apple ID and enable iCloud sync again. If iCloud sync was not enabled, the data stored locally on your device cannot be recovered after uninstalling.

### How do I set up feeding reminders?

Go to your pet's detail page, tap the feeding section, and configure your feeding schedule. You can set custom intervals and the App will send local notifications. You can also sync reminders to your system calendar.

### Does the app work offline?

Yes. ExoKeeper is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required for any feature.
