# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

ExoKeeper ("the App") is developed by Nan Zhang. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use ExoKeeper.

## Data Collection

**ExoKeeper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** All data you enter — including pet profiles, feeding records, temperature/humidity logs, growth records, medical records, expense records, breeding records, and photos — is stored exclusively on your device.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- If you are a Pro subscriber and enable iCloud sync, your data is synced through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is associated with your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us.
- We do not operate any servers and have no ability to access your data.

## Photos and Camera

The App may request access to your photo library and camera for the purpose of adding pet photos, recording shedding comparison photos, annotating images, and recording videos. These media files are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any external server.

## Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for feeding reminders, cleaning reminders, equipment replacement reminders, medical follow-up reminders, and hibernation schedule reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external service.

## Calendar Access

The App may request access to your system calendar (via EventKit) to sync feeding and cleaning schedules. This data stays within Apple's calendar system on your device and iCloud account.

## In-App Purchases

ExoKeeper offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple. We do not collect or have access to any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple's App Store.

## Third-Party Services

ExoKeeper does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, no personal data of any user — including children — is collected by us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
